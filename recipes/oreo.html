Deep-Fried Oreos

How to make Deep-Fried Oreos

These indulgent fried Oreos are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Milk
  • Pancake mix
  • Sugar
  • Oreos
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the batter
  2. Dip the cookies in the batter
  3. Fry the cookies in batches until they're golden brown
  4. Dust the finished Oreos with powdered sugar for a perfect presentation
